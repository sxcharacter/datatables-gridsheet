Single Cell

Copy/paste single cell with Ctrl+C / Ctrl+V

Paste data directly from Excel/Spreadsheet

Single Cell Copy/Paste

Click on the cell you want to copy

Cell will be highlighted with a blue border

Press Ctrl+C

Or Right click → Copy

Move to target cell

Use arrow keys or click on cell

Press Ctrl+V

Or Right click → Paste

Multi-Row Copy/Paste

Click No. column on the first row

Row will be highlighted in yellow

Hold Shift + click last row

Or drag to other rows. All rows in range will be selected

Press Ctrl+C

All row data will be copied to clipboard

Select target row and press Ctrl+V

Data will be pasted to those rows

Paste from Excel

Copy data in Excel

Select cells in Excel and press Ctrl+C

Click initial cell in GridSheet

This is the starting point for paste

Press Ctrl+V

Data will be pasted to subsequent columns automatically

Tip: Multi-column data from Excel will be automatically pasted to corresponding columns. Multi-row data will also be pasted to rows below.
