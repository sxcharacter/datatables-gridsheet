Selection Types

Type Indicator Activation Method
Single Cell Blue border Click any cell
Single Row Yellow background Click No. column
Multi-Cell Light blue background Drag from cell to cell
Multi-Row Multiple yellow rows Shift+Click or drag on No. column

Single Row Selection

With Mouse

Click Click number in No. column

Multi-Row Selection

With Mouse (Drag)

Drag Drag mouse from first row to last row in No. column

With Shift+Click

Click Row 1 Click first row in No. column
Shift + Click Row N Hold Shift, click last row

With Keyboard

Click Row Click first row in No. column
Shift + ↑↓ Extend selection with arrow keys

Multi-Cell Selection

With Mouse (Drag)

Drag Click and drag from start cell to end cell

With Shift+Click

Click Cell Click first cell
Shift + Click Hold Shift, click last cell

Keyboard Shortcuts

Shortcut Function
Arrow Keys Navigate between cells
Shift + Arrow Extend row selection
Shift + Click Extend selection to target
Escape Clear selection
